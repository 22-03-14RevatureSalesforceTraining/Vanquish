Strawberry Blueberry Yogurt Parfait With Granola

ingredients Measurements
vanilla yogurt 2 24-ounce (or 3 16-ounce) containers
honey 1/3 cup
blueberries (washed) 2 cups
Strawberries (washed and chopped) 4 cups
granola 4 cups
Equipment Needed
Pairing Knife
Cutting Board
Measuring Cups
2 Mixing Bowls
Cups, Mason Jars or Glass Jars
    Instructions
  1. In a mixing bowl, mix the yogurt and honey until well blended.
  2. In another bowl, combine the blueberries and chopped strawberries.
  3. Put 1/3 cup of the blueberry and strawberry mixture in the bottom of a cup, mason jar, or glass.
  4. Spoon 1/3 cup of the yogurt and honey mix on top of the fruit.
  5. Sprinkle 1/4 cup of granola on top of the yogurt.
  6. Repeat the fruit, yogurt and granola layers a second time.
  7. Serve immediately
