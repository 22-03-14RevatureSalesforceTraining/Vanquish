Minamoto Yoshitsune

Name of Weapons Attained

  1. Hizamaru
  2. Bishamon
  3. Hachiman
  4. Doujigiri
  5. Hidekiri and Usumidori

Famed hero of the late Heian period in Japan and star of the Heike Monogatari. Yoshitsune is the main character of Genji who wields dual katana, one in either hand. His fighting-style encompasses speed and agility in a graceful display of swordsmanship.

Musashibo Benkei

Name of Weapons Attained

  1. Benkei's Club
  2. Deva Club
  3. Dragon's Wrath
  4. Tendertusk
  5. Indomitable

Yoshitsune's loyal friend and self-appointed protector, most famous for his 'Standing Death'. In Genji, Benkei is a huge man with a giant club that swats most enemies like flies. Obviously, The brawn of the Genji group, Benkei uses slow yet powerful swings to decimate foes.

Shizuka Goze

Name of Weapons Attained

  1. Fleck
  2. Dancing Girl
  3. Wagtail
  4. Wind Wreath
  5. Lion Kagura

Shizuka uses a 'chain- blade'-type of weapon and fights mostly with wide, long- ranged sweeping attacks. She can zip around a battlefield, beating on enemies as she goes.

Lord Buson

Name of Weapons Attained

  1. Little Crow
  2. Fearsome Shichi
  3. Billowing Clouds
  4. Kurikara
  5. Sword of Susano-O

Not originating from the Heike Monogatari, or any other work of Japanese History or Legend, Buson is an original character created for the Genji storyline. He is a god that descends to earth and assumes the body of Taira no Kagekiyo; thus, he utilizes Kagekiyo's weapon and fighting style. Overall, he's kind of like a jack-of-all-trades, but not very good at anything.
